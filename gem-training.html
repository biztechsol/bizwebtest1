Training includes

A complete, practical curriculum

Every module is taught with real GeM screens, live examples, and hands-on exercises.

01

GeM Overview & Ecosystem

How the Government e-Marketplace works end-to-end: buyer categories (Central Ministries, State Departments, PSUs, Autonomous Bodies), seller/service-provider ecosystem, primary vs. reverse auction, DGS&D history, and where GeM fits in India's public procurement.

02

GeM Registration Process

Step-by-step live walkthrough of onboarding: Aadhaar OTP, PAN & GST validation, MSME/Udyam linkage, ITR of last three years, bank & ICEGATE details, primary user vs. secondary users, common rejection reasons and how to fix them.

03

Catalogue Listing (Products & Services)

Category mapping, brand approval process (OEM vs Reseller vs Non-branded), image standards, HSN codes, price schedule, warranty terms, MoQ/lot size and "Golden Rules" to make your listing findable and winnable.

04

Bid Search & Opportunity Filtering

Setting the right bid alerts, filtering by category, ministry, region, turnover eligibility, MSE/Startup preferences (PPP-MII), past-performance criteria — plus a repeatable framework to identify only bids you can realistically win.

05

Bid Submission Strategies

EMD/e-PBG basics, technical bid document pack, financial bid (unit price vs total), consortium/JV bids, exemptions available to MSMEs, and a live mock bid submission with feedback so you can bid confidently on your own.

06

Order Processing, Dispatch & Payments

Order acceptance workflow, PBG generation, dispatch documentation, invoice cycle, CRAC/PRC generation, GeM Sahay financing, dispute handling and reasons why payments get held — with fixes.

07

Post-training Guidance & Support

30 days of WhatsApp mentorship after training: ask any real-world question, share screenshots of stuck screens, get help with your first real bid. You are not sent off alone.
